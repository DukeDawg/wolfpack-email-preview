BEHIND THE PAGES

A Note from Holly Caste

“My newest release, Scarred Hearts is two books in one! It's an updated version of my debut duet: Blurry Messes and Messy Love. The story follows Ellie and Mateo as their romance unfolds while navigating college life and the hardships that come along with young adulthood. The premise of Scarred Hearts originally started out in my journal, as I reflected on a time in my life when I was dealing with similar issues as Ellie. I wanted this story to go deeper than a typical college romance and explore the complexities of emotional abuse, co-dependency and mental health and how it can impact one's concept of love. This story is an emotional rollercoaster but the journey toward self-worth and a thriving romance is worth the bumpy ride!”
