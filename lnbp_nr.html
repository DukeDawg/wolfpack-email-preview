Her

DV Fischer's Her is a scorching dark romance thriller from a USA Today bestselling author. A Billionaire's Heart

Love and Wealth 3

by Mika B.

Mika B's A Billionaire's Heart is the third book in the Love and Wealth series, a dark, spicy African American billionaire romance. Steven Smith is a hard man to love — ice-cold and impossible to tame, until he hires retired Naval officer Tisha McLean to keep him alive. Tisha has spent five years in love with a monster, watching Steven do nefarious things, and when an attempt on his life nearly succeeds she's forced to choose between what her head knows is right and what her heart wants. Power, control, and a bodyguard who may have to risk everything to protect the man she shouldn't love.
