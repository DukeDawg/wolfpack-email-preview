A Fear the Reaper story where one Reaper just is not enough. Obsidian wants to sell it, the Russians want it back, an arms manufacturer wants what is inside it, and extremist groups want what it could unleash. If you like movies on paper, this is one hell of a ride. I hope you enjoy this new Kane adventure.
