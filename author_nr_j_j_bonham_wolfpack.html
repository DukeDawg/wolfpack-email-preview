J.J. Bonham returns to Uzziah O'Bannon country with Crazy Loretta, a new mountain-man story built on suspicion, winter danger, and survival by hard instinct. Uzziah comes back to his cabins expecting silence, only to find a strange woman waiting, poison in the air, and a blizzard closing fast. For frontier readers who like trust measured carefully and danger waiting inside the door, this is the next hard trail.
