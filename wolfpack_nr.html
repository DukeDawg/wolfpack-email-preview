Featured release: Drygulch Country, plus Dead Man Walking. Available now.

Peter Brandvold leads this week’s Wolfpack new releases with Drygulch Country, the next Nordic & Finn ride. ★ Featured New Release ★

Drygulch Country

Nordic & Finn 4

by Peter Brandvold

When a battered girl stumbles out of the dark and asks for help, Anders Nordic gives his word. With Finn riding beside him, that oath pulls them into Beillorville, a canyon town ruled by a false preacher and the kind of evil that only yields to hard men, colder steel, and hotter lead.

From Peter Brandvold

“Hello the Fire! I have another entry in my Nordic & Finn series coming out May 5th — Drygulch Country. This series is especially close to my heart because it centers around a fella and a dog, that special relationship you know if you’re a man alone with a dog alone. Finn is especially poignant for me as I lost my own “Finn” — Buddy — around two months ago. But the Nordic & Finn series keeps both me and Buddy alive and together. You can’t ask much more than that.”

Dead Man Walking

Texas Ranger Rayland Gibbs 1

by Ash Lingam

Ranger Rayland Gibbs finds Captain Lynch Moon drygulched in the Chihuahua Desert, barely breathing and covered in blood. Saving Moon is only the first problem. The men who ambushed him are still out there, and Gibbs is the quiet sort who lets his guns do most of his talking.

From Ash Lingam

“I have something new in store for readers with Dead Man Walking. I have written an exciting, action-packed Texas Ranger tale. It’s not my first, but it may be my best. The story opens when Ranger Rayland Gibbs finds his boss, Captain Lynch Moon, drygulched in the Chihuahua Desert. Lynch Moon is the wrong man to leave alive, though. He’s harder to kill than a bad rumor. Rayland is the quiet sort who lets his guns do most of his talking. He kills seven men in seven minutes. And he’s just getting started.”
