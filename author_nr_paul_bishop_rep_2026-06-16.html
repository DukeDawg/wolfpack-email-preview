Hi there,

Deep Water is the second Calico Jack Walker / Tina Tamiko novel, and like Hot Pursuit, it's rooted in a specific LAPD reality. While preparing the book, I was following the controversy over the department's old practice of loading confiscated drugs and weapons onto a garbage scow and dumping them offshore. When whales beached along the Southern California coast with traces of PCP and other narcotics in their brains, the policy changed: stop dumping and start burning. But there was one last property barge scheduled before the new rule took effect. My writer's brain asked: what if that final floating arsenal, worth millions, was hijacked before it reached the ocean floor? That question put Calico, Tina, and everyone close to them in Deep Water. It's out now and free in Kindle Unlimited.
