The Healer

Brody Connor 3

by Monty R. Garner

Monty R. Garner's The Healer continues the Brody Connor classic western series. Brody has built a life with a wife, a newborn son, and a small hospital in Central Texas, until an outlaw gang targets his ranch and forces him to trade his medical bag for a gun. Guided by warrior teachings and faith, he uncovers a web of murder, stagecoach robbery, and the shadowy boss behind it all. A frontier adventure with heart and grit.

Words from Monty R. Garner

Dr. Brody built a small hospital beside his home so he could treat the country folk — helping people is the only reason he ever became a doctor. But when a ruthless outlaw gang threatens his wife and young child, he makes himself a promise: no one will harm the ones he loves. The Healer goes after them, and destruction follows. My fourth Brody Connor book, Promises Kept, arrives in July.

Loren D. Estleman's Western Collection

by Loren D. Estleman

Loren D. Estleman's Western Collection brings a Spur Award-winning master's western novels and stories together in one featured release. Built around outlaws, lawmen, and hard moral reckonings on the frontier, it delivers grit, glory, and gunsmoke from one of the West's greatest storytellers, the whole sweep of it in a single volume.

Words from Loren D. Estleman

I'll never get over the miracle of doing what I love and making a living at it. My mysteries built my house, but westerns will always be my first love. Page Murdock came about by accident — I'd conceived The High Rocks as a scalphunter's story, but by the end of the first chapter I realized he hadn't the vocabulary to speak for himself, and Murdock stepped into the frame to stay, through ten books and forty-seven years so far. Even the tales I spin from whole cloth are grounded in fact. Where else but in books can a man live other lives and other times than his own?
