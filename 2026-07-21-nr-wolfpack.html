Buffalo Bill’s Wild West Show

Uzziah Mountain Man 13

by J.J. Bonham

Uzziah O’Bannon and Immanuel leave the mountain trails for Buffalo Bill Cody’s Wild West Show in Europe, crossing paths with famous performers, royalty, and crowds eager to see the West become legend. Beneath the spectacle is a story of friendship, aging, and change.

Words from J.J. Bonham

In Buffalo Bill’s Wild West Show, I wanted to send Uzziah O’Bannon and Immanuel far beyond the mountain trails and into a world neither man expected: the grand spectacle of Buffalo Bill Cody’s Wild West Show on tour in Europe. Along the way, they cross paths with Annie Oakley, Sitting Bull, royalty, and crowds who have come to see the West turned into legend. But beneath the showmanship is a quieter story about friendship, aging, and the pain of watching a good man change. Uzziah hopes the journey will pull Immanuel back toward life. What he discovers is that some roads bring a man home different, and not every change can be fought with a gun or a strong will. Thanks for reading — J.J. Bonham

Promises Kept

Brody Connor 4

by Monty R. Garner

Brody Connor rides again in a classic Western about the promises that shape a man’s path. The fourth Brody Connor adventure brings frontier danger and hard-earned resolve to the next chapter of the series.

Words from Monty R. Garner

A doctor with a gun. A doctor never enjoys losing a patient, especially one who is taken by force from his hospital bed. Later, the Connor’s ranch hands find the patient murdered, lying in a gully. Dr. Brody only wanted to help people and let the law handle the outlaws. Killing their former member wasn’t enough for the outlaws. They returned to the doctor’s home and killed one of the ranch hands, who was considered family. The outlaws stepped over the line when they killed the ranch hand. Brody had made a promise to himself and others that he would let no one harm his family and get away with it. Promises Kept is a gripping story in the life of Dr. Brody. I recently returned from the mountains of Southern Colorado, where I started book five of the Dr. Brody Connor series. I also have a new Card Jordan book in my head, so I’ll be writing about Card later in the fall. I hope everyone has a great summer and stay safe. — Monty R. Garner
