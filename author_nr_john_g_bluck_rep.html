My new book is out this week: To Die in Livermore. My hometown inspired this seventh Luke Ryder mystery-thriller, a story of murder, espionage, and a nuclear-fusion rocket. I draw on years filming crime news in Washington, D.C., and three decades at NASA to imagine near-future crimes where advanced science turns dangerous. I hope you enjoy this flight of fancy into what may be just around the corner. It’s available now and free with Kindle Unlimited.
